SUMMARY: President Trump on Thursday told an audience of crypto founders and executives that he wants to make America the “undisputed bitcoin superpower and the crypto capital of the world.” In a pre-recorded speech, Trump underscored the U.S.'s commitment to dominating crypto and financial technologies.
