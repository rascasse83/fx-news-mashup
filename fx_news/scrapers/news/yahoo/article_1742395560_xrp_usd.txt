# XRP Price Surges After Ripple CEO Says the SEC Has Dropped Its Case. Bitcoin Is Also Rising.

Article ID: 16896e1d-b723-38cd-9a72-1f17ffe3bc29
Source: https://finance.yahoo.com/m/16896e1d-b723-38cd-9a72-1f17ffe3bc29/xrp-price-surges-after-ripple.html
Timestamp: 1742395560 (2025-03-19T14:46:00)

SUMMARY: XRP was soaring Wednesday after the CEO of Ripple Labs said that the Securities and Exchange Commission had dropped its long-running case against his company. XRP was racking up gains after Ripple CEO Brad Garlinghouse said in a video clip posted on X that the SEC had dropped its case against Ripple, bringing an end to a legal battle that dragged on for more than four years. An SEC spokesperson declined to comment when reached by Barron’s.
